 Step 1: Open App Registration

Navigate to the **App Registration** section within your Microsoft Entra ID.

![App Registration screen](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240430-103108.png "App Registration")

 Step 2: Select New Registration

Click on **New registration** to start the registration of a new application.

![New Registration](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240430-103521.png "New Registration")

 Step 3: Application Details

After registration, you will obtain a **Client ID** and **Client Secret**. Keep these details secure as they will be used in the following steps of your setup.

![Application Details](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240430-103836.png "Application Details")

 Step 4: Configure Optional Claims

Under **Optional Claims**, add necessary fields such as email. Ensure that Azure users have the respective details present in their account to utilize these fields effectively.

![Optional Claims](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240430-104251.png "Optional Claims")

 Step 5: Set Redirect URL in Authentication

Navigate to the **Authentication** section and fill in the redirect URL as required for your application.

![Authentication Redirect URL](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240430-104922.png "Authentication Redirect URL")

For further details and guidance, visit [this link](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/).

Ensure all settings are correctly configured and saved after making these changes.
