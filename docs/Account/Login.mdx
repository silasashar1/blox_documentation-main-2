# Login to Vue!

Welcome to Vue! You're on the verge of exploring something amazing. The first step, however, is to log in.

## Login Methods

Logging into the Vue App can be done through:

- **Email Credentials**
- **SSO** (Supported providers: Google and Okta)

Let's delve into each method for a clear understanding of how to access your Vue AI suite.
![Alt text](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/login1.png 'a title')

### Email-based Access

#### Login

If you already have Vue login credentials:

1. Navigate to the Login screen.
2. Enter your registered email address and password.
3. Click **‘Sign In’** to access your Vue AI suite.
![Alt text](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/login2.png 'a title')
#### Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click the **‘Forgot Password’** button on the Login screen.
2. Enter your registered email address and click **‘Send Reset Password Link’**.
3. Check your email for a link to reset your password.

![Alt text](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/login3.png 'a title')

#### Request Access

If you're new to Vue and don't have credentials:

- Click the **‘Contact Us’** button to request a demo and access credentials to the Vue AI suite.
![Alt text](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/login4.png 'a title')

### SSO-based Access

#### Activate Account

To activate your SSO login:

1. Have your company admin add your name and email through the **‘+ New User’** form within Account Settings.
2. Look for a Welcome email in your inbox with an authentication link.
3. Click the link to activate your account for a seamless sign-in experience.

#### Sign in

For signing in with activated SSO credentials:

1. Click on your SSO provider's logo.
2. Enter your credentials.
3. You're now logged into Vue!

![Alt text](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/login5.png 'a title')

This guide aims to make your login process as smooth as possible, ensuring you get to your AI suite with ease.
